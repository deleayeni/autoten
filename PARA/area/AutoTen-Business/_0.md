# AutoTen Business — Area

## What this Area tracks

The non-technical side of AutoTen: brand identity, business information, the 90-day roadmap from the original planning doc, success metrics, and the engagement model between you and your dad.

This is the **product/business layer** — the answers to "why does the site exist" and "what are we measuring." Separate from the technical layer (frontend code, data schema).

## Contents

```
_0.md                       ← you are here
brand-identity.md           — tagline, logo, brand colors, voice
business-info.md            — legal info, office, contact, socials
roadmap-90-day.md           — Phase 1/2/3 plan from the PDF
kpis.md                     — what to measure and why
engagement-and-pricing.md   — your role as Remote Head of Digital, pricing tiers
```

## Why these live in Areas, not Resources

Areas = ongoing concerns that need to stay current.
- Brand voice shifts → update `brand-identity.md`
- Dad opens a second location → update `business-info.md`
- KPIs start being tracked → `kpis.md` gets actual numbers
- Phase 1 ships → `roadmap-90-day.md` notes what's done

Resources are reference material you read. These are your **operating context** for running the digital side.
