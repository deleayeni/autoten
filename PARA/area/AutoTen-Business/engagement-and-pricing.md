# Engagement Model & Pricing

The proposed working arrangement between you and your dad for running AutoTen's digital operation. From the PDF.

## Proposed role

**Remote Head of Digital (Software Engineer)** — clear scope + KPIs + retainer.

### Scope

Owns end-to-end:
- Website + hosting
- Booking engine (rentals)
- Lead CRM
- Analytics + ad pixels
- Marketplace listings
- Automations (WhatsApp, SMS, email)
- Security posture

### Accountabilities

Everything in [kpis.md](./kpis.md). Monthly report showing leads, bookings, revenue impact.

## Pricing tiers (monthly retainer)

| Tier | Range (EUR) | Includes |
|---|---|---|
| **Lite** | €400–€700 | Hosting, updates, bugfixes, monthly KPI report |
| **Growth** | €900–€1,500 | Lite + ad campaigns, listing exports, A/B tests, CRM tweaks, content, 2 small features/month |
| **Full Stack** | €2,000–€3,500 | Growth + booking engine iterations, automation flows, ad ops, quarterly roadmap, priority support |

## Alternative / additive: project fees

One-off builds:

| Deliverable | Range (EUR) |
|---|---|
| Booking engine MVP | €1,800–€3,500 |
| CRM + admin dashboard | €1,200–€2,500 |
| Inventory intake + media pipeline | €900–€1,800 |
| Pixel/GA4 + reporting setup | €400–€900 |

## Alternative / additive: performance bonus

Stacks on top of a retainer:
- **€10–€25 per qualified lead**, OR
- **1–3% of rental revenue** attributed to online bookings

## Contract & operational basics

Per the PDF:

- **Statement of Work** with scope, SLAs (e.g. P1 bugs < 24h), and a change-request path
- **Ownership:** your dad owns the brand + content; you own the code IP but license it to the company (or transfer on final payment)
- **Access:** company GitHub / Vercel / Supabase projects under his org; you as maintainer
- **Data:** GDPR-aware consent, Nigerian payment compliance

I'll work on a fixed monthly fee plus an optional performance bonus. In the first month, I'll finish the site, connect inventory, and set up tracking so we can see results clearly."
