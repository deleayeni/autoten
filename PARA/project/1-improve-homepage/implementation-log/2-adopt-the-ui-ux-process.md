**Date:** 2026-04-21
**Agent:** Claude (Opus 4.7)

## The pivot

After Phase 1 shipped three hi-fi design previews (see [1-three-design-explorations.md](1-three-design-explorations.md)), Dele called out that we had skipped every earlier step of a real UI/UX process — no brief, no discovery, no personas, no IA, no content inventory, no moodboard, no wireframes. We jumped straight to step 8.

This log records the decision to stop and work the process correctly, rather than proceed to pick a winner between three aesthetic hypotheses that were built on no foundation.

## How the three preview routes are now treated

They are no longer "three competing directions, pick one." They are **research material** — evidence of three distinct aesthetic hypotheses, kept around so the moodboard step can reference them by screenshot and notes rather than building from zero. The winning final design may be one of them, a remix of them, or something the moodboard points to that none of them captured.

The preview routes stay live at `/design/option-a|b|c` until the real homepage ships. They get deleted together at the end of Phase 3.
